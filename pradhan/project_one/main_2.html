Our Story

It's easy to tell: Herbis Natura was born out of a passion for cooking, drinking tea and the ability to get something going online - a spice and herb shop that leaves nothing to be desired!It is very important to us: organic top quality!

Who where what?

Do you need a special spice? A tea? an herb? You are in the right place with us! We are a young team based in Berlin, specializing in the online delivery of natural and medicinal herbs, spices and teas in the best organic quality from certified retailers from all over the world.

Philosophy

As an eco-certified company, we support sustainable, healthy agriculture. 90% of our herbs come from controlled organic cultivation or wild growth. Convince yourself of the natural top quality and support the sustainability of our environment!

Our natural and medicinal herbs

We carry a high-quality range of over 300 different medicinal herbs. We cover a wide range, so everyone will find what they are looking for here. For the highest quality, we rely on controlled organic cultivation and gentle processing. If you still can't find a herb, talk to us!

Our spices and spice blends: Intense aromas

No matter what cuisine you eat best - Asian, local, Indian, Mediterranean or European - they all have one thing in common: without salt, pepper, herbs and spices, any dish will remain bland and far below its aromatic potential. That's why you can buy a number of particularly flavorful spices and spice mixtures from us. Almost all of them come from organic farming.

Our tea: from fruity-sweet to spicy-herbal

Hardly any force is stronger and more original than that of nature. In order to use them, teas and infusions are often used. Our tea category invites you to browse - from classic chamomile or peppermint tea to exotic varieties. How about Greek Verbena or Honeybush? In addition to tea made from medicinal herbs, our range also includes fruity-sweet to spicy-herbal tea blends with a delicious taste and no medicinal properties.
